Privacy policy

We collect the information you submit on forms (name, phone, email, stay preferences, messages) to respond to inquiries and complete bookings.

Payments are processed by Razorpay; we do not store full card or UPI credentials on our servers. Supabase may process and store booking records under your project configuration.

We may use analytics (such as Google Analytics) when configured, to understand traffic and improve the site.

To request access, correction or deletion of personal data tied to a booking, contact us using the details on the homepage.
